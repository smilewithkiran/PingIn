Our Story

The PingIN story

It started with a simple frustration — spending hours manually sending LinkedIn connection requests every day, while global tools charged thousands of rupees per month without understanding anything about the Indian market.

No tool knew the difference between Koramangala and Connaught Place. None had IIT or IIM filters. None accepted GPay. None had WhatsApp support in Indian hours.

So we built PingIN — starting from scratch, designed exclusively for India's 140 million LinkedIn users. Every feature was built with Indian professionals in mind — from the GCC company filter to the layoff-affected profile search to the ₹650 for 15 days price point that makes it accessible to every recruiter.

We believe India deserves world-class talent search engines at Indian prices. That's PingIN. 🇮🇳
