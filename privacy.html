PingIN (pingin.in) is operated by PingIN Technologies, Hyderabad, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, Chrome Extension, and campaign tools.

3. Chrome Extension — Important

The PingIN Chrome Extension operates entirely within your browser. This means:

  • All LinkedIn profiles you scrape are stored in your browser's local storage — not on our servers
  • Your LinkedIn session cookie (li_at) is never transmitted to PingIN servers
  • Campaign data, messages, and profile lists exist only on your device
  • We cannot access, view, or retrieve your scraped profile data

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your PingIN account
  • To process payments and activate subscriptions
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, activation, password reset)
  • To provide customer support via email and WhatsApp
  • To improve PingIN features and fix bugs
  • To send product updates (you can unsubscribe anytime)

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — payment processing (their privacy policy applies to payment data)
  • Email service providers — for transactional emails only
  • Legal authorities — if required by Indian law

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to personal data. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong password and keep your account credentials safe.

7. Your Rights

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update incorrect information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email us at support@pingin.in.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings — some features may not work correctly without them.

9. Third-Party Links

PingIN may contain links to LinkedIn and other third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

PingIN is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or a notice on pingin.in. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
