Quick Answers

My extension is not working?
Go to chrome://extensions, find PingIN, click the reload icon. If it still fails, email support@pingin.in with a screenshot.
License key not activating?
Check your email inbox and spam folder. Copy the key exactly — no extra spaces. Email us if it still doesn't work.
Payment done but plan not active?
Email support@pingin.in with your payment screenshot and registered email. We activate within 2 hours.
Will PingIN get my LinkedIn account banned?
PingIN limits sends to 50/day with natural pacing. Start with 20/day for the first week to be extra safe.
