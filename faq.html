🚀 Getting Started
How do I install PingIN?
+
PingIN is a Chrome Extension. Here's how to install it:
  • Download your plan's ZIP file from your Dashboard
  • Unzip the file on your computer
  • Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  • Enable Developer mode (toggle in top-right corner)
  • Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder
  • PingIN appears in your Chrome toolbar — pin it for easy access
The whole process takes under 2 minutes.
Does PingIN work with a free LinkedIn account?
+
Yes! PingIN works with a completely free LinkedIn account. You do NOT need LinkedIn Premium or LinkedIn Recruiter. PingIN enhances what a free LinkedIn account can already do — it just automates and speeds it up dramatically.
Will LinkedIn ban or restrict my account?
+
PingIN is designed to stay within LinkedIn's safe usage limits:
  • Maximum 50 connection requests per day (LinkedIn's informal safe limit is ~100)
  • All actions are paced naturally — not instant rapid-fire
  • No fake profiles, no scraping against ToS
Best practices to stay safe: Start with 20 requests/day for your first week, then increase gradually. Avoid sending all 50 to the same company in one day. Keep your LinkedIn profile complete and professional.

Note: LinkedIn's terms prohibit automated tools. While PingIN users have used it safely, we cannot guarantee LinkedIn will never flag your account. Use responsibly.
Can I use PingIN on multiple computers?
+
Your license key can be used on one computer at a time. If you switch computers, simply install the extension on the new machine and enter the same license key. To use on multiple computers simultaneously, you would need multiple licenses.
Does PingIN work on mobile?
+
PingIN is a Chrome desktop extension — it works only on Chrome on Windows, Mac, and Linux computers. It does not work on mobile Chrome or any mobile browser. The website (pingin.in) works on mobile for account management and dashboard access.

🔌 Extension Features
What is Smart Search and how does it work?
+
Smart Search lets you search LinkedIn by job title, company, city, experience level, and college in one go. You can add multiple companies as chips (press Enter after each). PingIN builds the optimal LinkedIn search URL and opens it, then automatically collects all matching profiles into your list.

It searches across 886+ Indian companies and 60+ top colleges including IIT, NIT, IIM, BITS, and more.
How does AI Clone Search work?
+
Clone Search Pro / Annual lets you paste any LinkedIn profile URL. PingIN reads that person's headline, role, and education, then uses AI to find 100 similar profiles on LinkedIn. Perfect for when you've just made a great hire and want to find more like them.
Is my LinkedIn password stored by PingIN?
+
No — never. PingIN does not ask for, store, or transmit your LinkedIn password. It works by reading the LinkedIn pages you're already viewing in your browser — the same way you'd read them yourself. Your LinkedIn login session remains entirely in your browser.
What does "50 messages per day" mean?
+
On paid plans, PingIN allows up to 50 automated connection requests or messages per day. This counter resets every 24 hours. The free trial allows 5 per day. This limit is enforced to keep your LinkedIn account safe and avoid LinkedIn rate-limiting your account.
Can I export collected profiles?
+
Yes Starter and above. From the Profiles tab in the extension, click the Export CSV button to download all collected profiles as a spreadsheet. The CSV includes name, headline, location, profile URL, and any notes you've added. You can open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet tool.
🔧 Technical
Why does Chrome show a warning when installing?
+
Because PingIN is currently installed as an "unpacked extension" (not yet on the Chrome Web Store), Chrome shows a warning: "This extension can read and change your data on linkedin.com."

This is expected and safe. PingIN needs to read LinkedIn pages to collect profile data — that's exactly what it does, and only on linkedin.com. It cannot read any other website. We are in the process of submitting PingIN to the Chrome Web Store for official listing.
PingIN is not finding any profiles — what do I do?
+
Try these steps:
  • Make sure you're logged in to LinkedIn in the same Chrome browser
  • Try a broader search — remove college or experience filters first
  • Check that your LinkedIn account is in good standing (no restrictions)
  • Reload the extension: go to chrome://extensions, find PingIN, click the reload icon
  • Try a different search — some company names need exact spelling
Still not working? Email support@pingin.in with a screenshot.
My license key isn't working — what do I do?
+
First, check your email inbox and spam folder for the license key email. Make sure you're copying the key exactly — no extra spaces. If the issue persists, email support@pingin.in with your registered email address and payment screenshot and we'll sort it out immediately.
